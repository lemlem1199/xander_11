Chess is a fun and challenging game where two players compete to checkmate each other's king.
Here are the main objectives in chess:

1. Checkmate the King: The ultimate goal is to trap your opponent's king so it can't move without being captured.
2. Protect Your King: Keep your own king safe by moving pieces to safe place.
3. Capture Opponent’s Pieces: Take your opponent’s pieces off the board to weaken their position
4. Control the Board: Use your pieces to control important squares and limit your opponent’s movements.
5. Develop Your Pieces: Move your knights, bishops, rooks, and queen to active positions early in the game to set up for attacks.
